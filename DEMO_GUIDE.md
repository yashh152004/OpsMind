# OpsMind Enterprise: Professional Demonstration Guide

This guide is designed for recruiters, hiring managers, and enterprise stakeholders. It outlines the narrative and technical highlights of the OpsMind observability platform.

## Narrative Overview
OpsMind is a next-generation observability platform designed for high-density SRE (Site Reliability Engineering) workflows. It focuses on **Operational Intelligence**, moving beyond simple metrics to **Autonomous Reasoning**.

---

## Part 1: Executive Intelligence (Dashboard)
**Goal:** Show how leadership and SRE leads get an instant "Heartbeat" of the organization.

- **KPI Matrix:** Highlight the SLA/SLO adherence and live error rates. Note the precise "Metric Mono" typography for readability.
- **AI Operational Reasoning:** Point out the "Intelligence Unit" table. This isn't just an alert log; it's a reasoning chain showing *what* happened, *why*, and the *confidence* level of the AI.
- **Resource Trends:** Show the unified telemetry charts that combine CPU, Memory, and Network into a single high-density view.

## Part 2: Incident Command & Triage
**Goal:** Demonstrate the formal, high-stakes environment of incident response.

- **The Incident HUD:** Show the tabbed interface (Investigating, Mitigating, etc.) which mimics real-world SRE tooling like PagerDuty or incident.io.
- **Declaration Flow:** Trigger the "Declare Incident" modal. Emphasize the professional fields: Severity Tiers (P1-P3) and Service Context.
- **Action Toolbar:** Point to the "AI Analysis" button that links the incident directly to the autonomous reasoning console.

## Part 3: Alert Stream & Telemetry
**Goal:** Show real-time signal processing and "noise reduction."

- **The Live Stream:** Use the **Demo Controller** (Scenario Lab) to inject a "Database Crash."
- **Recognition:** Watch how the Alert Stream catches the signal instantly.
- **Contextual Data:** Show the source icons (K8s, DB, Network) and how they provide immediate metadata for the operator.

## Part 4: Infrastructure Asset Management
**Goal:** Demonstrate enterprise-scale resource tracking.

- **Inventory View:** Show the multi-cloud resource list. Highlight the health index bars and regional markers.
- **Stability Monitoring:** Point out the "Platform Availability" KPI (99.99%)—showing that the system tracks its own uptime metrics.

## Part 5: The AI SRE Agent (Chat)
**Goal:** Showcase the unique competitive advantage.

- **Direct Interaction:** Navigate to the AI Chat.
- **Professional Prompting:** Show how the system understands SRE commands like `/rca` (Root Cause Analysis) or `/analyze asset`.
- **Reasoning Display:** Highlight how the AI doesn't just "chat" but returns structured evidence and suggested remediation steps.

---

## Technical Highlights for Engineers
- **Design System:** Built with a custom Navy/Slate enterprise CSS system for maximum density and low eye strain.
- **State Management:** Powered by `@tanstack/react-query` for robust server-state synchronization.
- **Real-time Engine:** Utilizes custom hooks and SSE/Polling (simulated) for 100ms-latency signal updates.
- **Typography:** Uses *Inter* for UI and *JetBrains Mono* for telemetry data to ensure zero ambiguity in production environments.
